subrelation (subrelation)

a transitive meta-level relation between two relations r1 and r2, such that if r1 holds between a and b (optionally: t) then r2 must hold between a and b (t)

Examples

  • agent_in subrelation participates_in [RO]
  • proper_part_of subrelation part_of [RO]
  • negatively_regulates subrelation regulates [GO: Every instance of negative regulation is necessarily an instance of regulation]
