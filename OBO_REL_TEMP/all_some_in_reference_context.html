all_some_in_reference_context (all_some_in_reference_context)

relates a type-level relation tr to its instance-level counterpart ir, such that for two types, A and B, related by tr, it is the case that all instances of A stand in a ir relation to some B where both instances stand in relation r2 to the same entity
