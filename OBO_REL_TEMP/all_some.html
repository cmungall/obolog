all_some (all_some)

relates a type-level relation tr to its instance-level counterpart ir, in an atemporal way, such that for two types, A and B, related by tr, it is the case that all instances of A are related by ir to some instance of B

""

Examples

  • all_some( part_of_some, part_of) and part_of_some( mitosis, M_phase_of_mitotic_cell_cycle) [RO]
